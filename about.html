Who We Are

About Us

A Story of Unity & Purpose

The Community Connect Group was founded on the belief that when people come together - regardless of background, age, or fitness level - extraordinary things happen. We are a registered community organisation dedicated to fostering personal growth, financial wellness, and lasting social bonds among our members and the wider community.

Our Mission

To build a strong, inclusive community where every member feels valued, supported, and empowered to achieve their personal and financial goals.

Our Vision

A thriving community where mutual support, shared savings, and collective wellness create lasting positive change for all members and their families.

Our Values

Integrity, inclusivity, accountability, and compassion guide every decision we make - from organising events to managing our community savings pool.

Founded

Established in 2018, we have grown from a small circle of neighbours into a vibrant organisation with over 200 active members across the region.
